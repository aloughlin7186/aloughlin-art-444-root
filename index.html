About

Hello! I'm Abi “Gaelica” Loughlin, a ceramicist and graphic designer. While the two fields are very different, I have countless hours of experience working in both. I primarily focus on animal form and will incorporate them both into my sculptural and design work. I aim for rich storytelling and immersive experiences withing my work as a whole. I’m born and raised in San Diego, and I’m in my final semester at SDSU and graduating Spring 2025. Scroll down to see my skills and work!
